Introduction

   Deep Learning Laboratory of Peking University is the most important basic research institute. It brings together Peking University's most active academics in the fields of computer science, applied mathematics and computational mathematics. The laboratory director is Professor Zhang Zhihua, one of the most active young scholars in the field of machine learning.
   The director of deep learning laboratory group 26 is Zhanxin Zhu. He is currently a research scientist in Peking University (北京大学) and Beijing Institute of Big Data Research (北京大数据研究院). He obtained his PhD in machine learning from School of Informatics of University of Edinburgh, UK. His research interests cover methodology of machine learning and artificial intelligence and their applications in various areas.
1. Methodogy: deep learning, reinforcement learning, scalable optimization and Bayesian inference methods.
2. Applications: prediction problems in traffic, data-driven medical imaging, generative models and reinforcement learning for computer graphics and network security.
